## 🎯 **Design Philosophy**

### Spacing
✅ Each element gets its own screen  
✅ Plenty of breathing room  
✅ Never more than 1-2 sentences visible  
✅ Progressive revelation builds intrigue

### Typography Hierarchy
1. **TARA logo text:** Largest (9rem)
2. **Main statements:** Large (8rem → 6rem)
3. **Features:** Medium (6rem → 5rem)
4. **Opening question:** Comfortable reading (3rem)
5. **CTA:** Clear but not overwhelming (5rem)

### Colors
- **Primary text:** White (#fff)
- **Secondary text:** Gray (#9ca3af)
- **Accents:** Blue glow effects
- **Background:** Pure black (#000)

### Animations
- **Fade in:** 0.8s as you arrive
- **Fade out:** 0.8s as you leave
- **Glow breathing:** 3s cycle
- **Particles:** Constant gentle movement

---

## 💡 **Why This Flow Works**

1. **Opens with relatability** - The phone question connects emotionally
2. **Introduces brand** - TARA appears dramatically with living effects
3. **Builds mystery** - "Something intelligent" (but what?)
4. **Reveals promise** - What TARA actually is
5. **Teases features** - Three core capabilities
6. **Completes message** - "is awakening" payoff
7. **Captures interest** - Clean CTA to join

**Result:** A journey that's mysterious, engaging, and never overwhelming!
