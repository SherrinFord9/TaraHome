## 💡 Design Principles

### Readability
- Line height: 1.5-1.75 (comfortable reading)
- Letter spacing: Wide tracking for mysterious feel
- Font weight: Light (300) for elegance

### Centering
- All sections: `flex items-center justify-center`
- All text: `text-center`
- All containers: `mx-auto` (auto margins)

### Spacing
- Section height: 100vh (full screen)
- Padding: 1.5rem (24px) on sides
- Max width: 672px (prevents long lines)
